What is twainers?

Twainers helps you to find training partners near you for your sport who are at the same level as you.

Why train with a partner?

Training with a partner is proven to help you reach your goals faster and keep you motivated.

Why create twainers?

We created twainers as we needed some extra motivation to get us off the couch during winter. Download the app now and start training today!
